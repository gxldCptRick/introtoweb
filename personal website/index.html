About Me

I am a 19 year old programmer currently studying Computer Science at Neumont College Of Computer Science. I have had the most amazing time meeting new people and creating new friendships. I have studied programming for many years first learning c# way back in 2014. I remember having a hard time understanding the concept of GUIs and switch cases way back in the day.

I always needed to look at like 20 different examples in order for me to understand what I was doing. I have lately read a book that has changed the way I think. It is called The Clean Coder by Robert C. Martin and it has helped me understand what it means to be a professional programmer . One of my favorite quotes from the book is Your career is your responsibility - Robert C. Martin. This means a lot to me because what he is saying is that your success is ultimaltey based on your effort. Most people take that phrase for granted and that think that the world is gonna gift them oppurtnities but that is not nor has ever been the case. I am proud to be a student at Neumont and I am so happy to be a part of this wonderful school.
